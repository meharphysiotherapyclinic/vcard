About Dr. Arneja

Dr. Govindpreet Singh Arneja (B.P.T., M.I.A.P.) is a Senior Physiotherapist with over 20 years of clinical experience in Greater Noida West.

At Mehar Physiotherapy Clinic, he provides specialized care for:

  • Neuro Rehab: Stroke, Paralysis, Parkinson’s, Bell’s Palsy.
  • Orthopedic: Slip Disc, Sciatica, Frozen Shoulder, Back/Neck Pain.
  • Advanced Care: Sports Injury & Post-Surgical Rehabilitation.

Utilizing Manual Therapy and PNF techniques, Dr. Arneja delivers personalized, one-on-one treatment to restore your mobility and confidence.
